Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about contacting and working with Orbicure.

How quickly will I receive a response to my inquiry?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. Urgent inquiries are prioritized and typically receive a response within 2 hours. For emergency medical inquiries, please use our 24/7 emergency line.

What information should I include in my inquiry?

Please include your full name, contact information, organization (if applicable), and specific details about your inquiry. For product-related questions, mention the product name if known. The more details you provide, the better we can assist you.

Can healthcare professionals request samples or literature?

Yes, registered healthcare professionals can request product samples, clinical literature, and educational materials. Please contact our medical affairs department with your professional credentials and specific requests.

What are your business hours?

Our main office is open Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM PKT, and Saturdays from 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM PKT. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays. Our emergency support line operates 24/7 for urgent medical inquiries.

How can I become an Orbicure distributor?

We welcome inquiries from qualified distributors. Please contact our business development team with your company profile, distribution capabilities, territory information, and relevant experience in pharmaceutical distribution. Our team will guide you through the partnership process.
